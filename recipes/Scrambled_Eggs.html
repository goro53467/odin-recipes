Scrambled Eggs

Description

Delicious scrambled eggs. Yum!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs per person
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • Fine grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 OXO cube, crushed
  • Salt (pinch)
  • Pepper (pinch)
  • Chilli powder (pinch)

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into a deep frying pan
  2. Wash your hands
  3. Grate in the cheese and add a slice of butter
  4. Crumble in the OXO cube
  5. Add a pinch of salt, pepper and chilli powder
  6. Heat over a low heat, stirring continuously
  7. Remove from the heat when it appears 90% done
