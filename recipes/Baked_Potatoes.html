Baked Potatoes

Description

Tasty, scrummy baked potatoes!

Ingredients

  • 1-2 baking potatoes per person
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Handful of salt
  • Butter (for serving)
  • Cheddar cheese (for serving) (to be grated)

Directions

  1. Cover the baking potatoes with olive oil and salt
  2. Microwave the potatoes for 12 minutes plus 2 minutes per potato
  3. Transfer potatoes to oven for 20 minutes to crisp
  4. Serve with butter and grated cheddar cheese!
